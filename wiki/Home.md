# Welcome to the Deep Research Agent Wiki

The **Deep Research Agent** is a sophisticated autonomous research assistant built on top of [LangGraph](https://github.com/langchain-ai/langgraph). Unlike standard RAG systems, it doesn't just retrieve information; it iteratively investigates, validates, and synthesizes knowledge.

We believe that AI research should be **transparent**, **iterative**, and **verifiable**. This agent is designed to:
1.  **Understand** the nuance of a user's request.
2.  **Strategize** the best way to uncover hidden information.
3.  **Validate** findings by looking for gaps.
4.  **Collaborate** with humans via an "Interrupt & Review" pattern.
